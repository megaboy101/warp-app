Basic Usage:

  1. Open two tabs
  2. Open the console with both tabs (Crtl+Shift+i). You should see a magic word fetched for you.
  3. FOR THE RECEIVING TAB: Enter in the SENDERS magic word into the input bar and click "connect"
  4. FOR THE SENDING TAB: Click to "choose a file", then click "send"
  5. A link should now have appeared in the receivers tab. Click it to download the senders file
